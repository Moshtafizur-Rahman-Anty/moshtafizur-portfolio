How to Fix WooCommerce Product Image Not Displaying

One common issue in WooCommerce is when product images don’t display properly on the product page. This can be frustrating for store owners, as images are crucial for showcasing products. Here’s a quick fix that often resolves this issue.

Solution Steps:
  1. Check Theme Compatibility: Ensure your theme is compatible with WooCommerce and is up-to-date.
  2. Regenerate Thumbnails: Use a plugin like "Regenerate Thumbnails" to regenerate all product image sizes.
  3. Update WooCommerce and Plugins: Ensure WooCommerce and related plugins are updated.
  4. Check File Permissions: Ensure media upload permissions are set correctly (755 for folders, 644 for files).
  5. Clear Cache: Clear your cache if you’re using a caching plugin.

By following these steps, you should be able to resolve the issue of missing product images on WooCommerce.

How to Fix the "Cart is Empty" Issue in WooCommerce

A frustrating issue for WooCommerce store owners and customers alike is when the cart appears empty after adding items, preventing checkout. Here’s how to troubleshoot and resolve this issue.

Solution Steps:
  1. Clear Cache: Clear your site’s and browser’s cache. If you use a caching plugin, ensure it’s configured to exclude WooCommerce cart and checkout pages.
  2. Enable Cookies: WooCommerce requires cookies to store cart data. Ensure cookies are enabled in your browser and on your server.
  3. Check Session Settings: WooCommerce relies on PHP sessions to maintain cart data. Ensure your server supports sessions and they are properly configured in your PHP settings.
  4. Disable Plugins: Deactivate other plugins one by one to check if a plugin conflict is causing the issue. Some caching or optimization plugins may disrupt cart functionality.
  5. Test with Default Theme: Switch temporarily to a default WordPress theme (like Twenty Twenty-One) to see if your theme is causing the issue.

By following these steps, you should be able to resolve the "cart is empty" issue and ensure a smooth shopping experience for your customers. For further help, consult WooCommerce documentation or reach out to your web host.
